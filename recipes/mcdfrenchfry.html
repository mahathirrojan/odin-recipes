Here's how to make french fries from McDonald's.

Ingredients:

  • Shoestring frozen fries
  • Cooking Stock (Beef flavored)
  • Aluminum free baking powder
  • Blend of canola oil and soybean oil, with corn oil mixed in

Directions:

  1. Make the brine:

    • Add a quarter cup of ice to a measuring cap.
    • To that, add a half cup of unsalted beef stock.
    • Add a quarter cup of soy milk, and a half teaspoon of baking powder. Give it a little twirl.

  2. Dunk your fries:

    • Drop your fries and turn them over once
    • Quickly evacuate them from the brine and put them on a flat tray and park them back in the freezer for five minutes.

  3. The fry:

    • Get your oil to 375 degree Fahrenheits and drop your fries and cook for 3 minutes and ten seconds.
    • However, everyone's fryers are differnet so make sure to test out in a little batch and find the perfect time, mine was 2 minutes.
    • When they're done, take them out and salt them.

Enjoy your McDonald's fries!
