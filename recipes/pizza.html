How to make Chicken Parmesan Pizza:

Prep and Cook times:

    Prep Time: 5 minutes
    Cook time: 18 minutes
    Total time: 23 minutes
    Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 12 frozen chicken nugget
  • 1 12-inch pre-bake pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 buttons mushroom, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 6 basil leaves, cut into thin ribbons

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Arrange nuggets in a single layer on a pizza pan.
  2. Bake nuggets in the pre-heated oven for 10 minutes or according to package directions; transfer to a cutting board and cut each nugget in half.
  3. Increase the oven temperature to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  4. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until heated through, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and scatter basil over cheese; bake for 3 minutes more.
